Concepción Heritage - Website Planning Document

Site Name

Concepción Heritage

This site highlights the rich history, vibrant culture, and iconic landmarks of my hometown, Concepción, Chile. The name reflects a celebration of local heritage and identity.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to promote tourism and local pride in Concepción by sharing its historical milestones, cultural traditions, and must-visit locations. The site will include sections about history, gastronomy, music, and nature trails around the Biobío region.

Scenarios

  • Where can tourists find the most iconic historical landmarks in Concepción?
  • What are some local traditions and festivals unique to Concepción and the Biobío region?

Color Schema

The color palette is inspired by the flag of Concepción:

#003366
Blue
#FFCC00
Yellow
  • Blue (#003366): Used for headings, borders, and navigation bar.
  • Yellow (#FFCC00): Used for highlights, buttons, and accent details.
  • White background: Used to maintain clarity and contrast.

Typography

  • Poppins (Sans-serif): Used for all text. Bold for headings, regular for paragraphs, ensuring readability and modern appearance.
